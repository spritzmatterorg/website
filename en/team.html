Our Team

Spritz Matter is a spin-off from the University of Padova, led by Mauro Conti, full professor and creator of the research group SPRITZ. Spritz Matter is composed of young talents with a strong academic background and a deep spirit of innovation. Our operational team members have earned master's degrees and PhDs in Cybersecurity or Artificial Intelligence, acquiring the skills to stay updated on emerging technologies and leverage them to the fullest. This unique combination of academic expertise and business acumen enables us to provide innovative and cutting-edge solutions for the most complex challenges.
