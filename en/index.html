Who we are

Researchers and experts in the field

Spritz Matter is a spin-off of the University of Padua, specializing in Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence. Founded by Professor Mauro Conti, creator of the SPRITZ Research Group. Its goal is to transfer years of academic knowledge into the industrial sector, providing cutting-edge solutions to companies.
